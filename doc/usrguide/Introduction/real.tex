\subsection{Real Attributes}

Most attributes are of type REAL and are treated internally as double
precision values. They may be set by integer values, real values,  or
expressions. 

Example:  
\begin{verbatim}
ddd:   drift, l = 1.2345;
dddd:  drift, l = ddd->l-0.3;
\end{verbatim}
