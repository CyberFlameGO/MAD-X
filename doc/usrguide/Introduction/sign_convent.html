Note the factorial in the denominator. The field coefficients have the following meaning:

  • B0: Dipole field, with a positive value in the positive y direction; a positive field bends a positively charged particle to the right.
  • B1: Quadrupole coefficient

    B1 = (del By / del x);

    a positive value corresponds to horizontal focussing of a positively charged particle.

  • B2: Sextupole coefficient

    B2 = (del2 By / del x2).

  • B2: Octupole coefficient

    B3 = (del3 By / del x3).
