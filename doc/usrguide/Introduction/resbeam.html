RESBEAM: reset beam defaults

label: RESBEAM,SEQUENCE=name;
If the sequence name is omitted, the default beam is reset. 
Default BEAM Data:
PARTICLE        POSITRON
ENERGY          1 GeV
EX              1 rad m
EY              1 rad m
ET              1 rad m
KBUNCH          1
NPART           0
BCURRENT        0 A
BUNCHED         .TRUE.
RADIATE         .FALSE.
