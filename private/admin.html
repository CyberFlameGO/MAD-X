Releases

To rollback a MAD-X release (dev or pro), change the symbolic links last-dev or last-pro on lxplus in ~mad/madx/releases. Do not change anything else because many external links use these symbolic links to select the path to the proper releases.

E-mail & E-groups

  • mad@cern.ch (alias for support), mad.support@cern.ch
  • mad-pub@cern.ch: public announcement (~250 persons, archived)
  • mad-usr@cern.ch: users mailing list (~100 persons, archived)
  • mad-dev@cern.ch: developpers mailing list (~15 persons, archived)
  • mad-trc@cern.ch: dispatch ticket reports from Trac
  • mad-src@cern.ch: dispatch commit reports from SVN
  • mad-sup@cern.ch: archive mad@cern.ch incoming emails
Note: when replying with the mad@cern.ch email account, don't forget to put mad in CC to your reply, otherwise other MAD-team members will not see your post and it will not be archived (only incoming emails are archived).
